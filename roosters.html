Eager to Return

After months of being sidelined due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the Roosters finally return to the court for a scrimmage against the Baltimore Cannons.


Many of the players had a difficult time getting back into a competitive level of play but they know that with a little conditioning they will be game-ready in no time.

Diallo Shines in Scrimmage

Not all players were ready to return to the hardwood after a few months away, but one player in particular appeared as if he never missed a day away from the game.


Providence guard, Alpha Diallo was dazzling on the court today; scoring 24 points to go along with three assists and 7 rebounds. Teammates and coaches alike were impressed with the performance.

Fun for the Fans

The Providence Roosters held a special event last night for their loyal fans. Prior to their return to action with a scrimmage against the Baltimore Cannons, the Roosters held an intra-squad scrimmage that included a skills competition, a 3-point competition and a dunk contest.


Fans in attendance were treated to an autograph session after the event. The event was held to show the appreciation that the Rooster organization has for the fans that have shown so much support to the team throughout the pandemic.
